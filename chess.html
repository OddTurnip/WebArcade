Quick Rules

  • King moves 1 square any direction - protect it!
  • Queen moves any distance in any direction
  • Rook moves horizontally/vertically
  • Bishop moves diagonally
  • Knight moves in L-shape, can jump pieces
  • Pawn moves forward, captures diagonally
  • Castling: King + Rook swap (if neither has moved)
  • Checkmate the opponent's King to win!
